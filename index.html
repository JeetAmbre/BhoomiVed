About Us

BhoomiVed is a student-led initiative dedicated to solving real-world environmental and urban challenges through data, research, technology, innovation, and awareness. Coming from diverse backgrounds, we combine geospatial analysis, environmental science, policy insights, and on-ground observations to build solutions that matter.

Our mission is to understand our ecosystems better — and empower communities and cities with actionable insights that create meaningful change.

Upcoming Projects

Project CoolCity — A Data-Driven Approach to Understanding Urban Heat in MMR

Urban areas are warming rapidly due to the Urban Heat Island effect. Project CoolCity aims to build a device to measure heat locally, analyse satellite and on-ground data, develop a prediction model for heat hotspots and heatwaves, and suggest cooling solutions aligned with UN SDGs.
